Our Story

More than just coffee

Fuchhay Coffee was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can change the pace of your entire day. We source our beans directly from local Cambodian farms, roast them in small batches, and serve every cup with care.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a long afternoon with a book, we want you to feel at home.
